Quick Healthy Meals

Grilled Chicken Power Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Brown rice
  • Avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Spinach

Recipe:

Cook the rice, grill the chicken with light seasoning, slice avocado and vegetables. Combine everything in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil.

Protein Oatmeal Bowl

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk or almond milk
  • Protein powder
  • Banana
  • Peanut butter

Recipe:

Cook oats with milk, stir in protein powder, top with banana slices and a spoon of peanut butter.

Baked Salmon & Veggies

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillet
  • Broccoli
  • Sweet potato
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Recipe:

Bake salmon at 180°C for 15–20 minutes. Roast sweet potatoes and broccoli with olive oil. Serve with lemon juice.

High-Protein Lean Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • Lean beef patty (5% fat)
  • Whole grain bun
  • Lettuce & tomato
  • Low-fat cheese
  • Greek yogurt sauce

Recipe:

Grill the beef patty, toast the bun, layer with veggies and cheese. Replace mayo with Greek yogurt sauce for a healthier option.

Grilled Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken strips
  • Corn tortillas
  • Avocado
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Salsa

Recipe:

Warm tortillas, fill with grilled chicken, top with avocado and salsa. Light, high-protein, and perfect for muscle gain.

High-Protein Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Whole wheat pizza base
  • Grilled chicken
  • Low-fat mozzarella
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bell peppers

Recipe:

Spread sauce, add toppings, bake at 200°C for 12–15 minutes. High protein, lower guilt.

Protein Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • Oats (blended)
  • Eggs
  • Protein powder
  • Milk
  • Blueberries

Recipe:

Blend ingredients into batter, cook on medium heat for 2–3 minutes per side. Top with berries and a drizzle of honey.

Chocolate Peanut Butter Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate protein powder
  • Banana
  • Peanut butter
  • Almond milk
  • Ice cubes

Recipe:

Blend everything for 30 seconds until smooth. Great post-workout recovery drink.
