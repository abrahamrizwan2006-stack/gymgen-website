Workout Guide

Learn more about each muscle group and the exercises that target them.

Click on a muscle group below to see some of the most effective exercises.

  • Barbell Bench Press – Best overall chest exercise for building strength
  • Dumbbell Bench Press – Helps improve balance and control
  • Incline Bench Press – Focuses more on the upper chest
  • Chest Dips – Good bodyweight movement for chest and triceps
  • Cable Flyes – Often used as a finishing exercise
  • Deadlifts – Builds overall strength and works the whole back
  • Pull-Ups – Great for increasing back width
  • Barbell Rows – Adds thickness to the back
  • Lat Pulldowns – Easier alternative to pull-ups
  • Seated Cable Rows – Allows controlled back movement
  • Close-Grip Bench Press – Targets triceps strength
  • Barbell Curls – Basic exercise for bicep growth
  • Skull Crushers – Isolates the triceps
  • Hammer Curls – Works biceps and forearms
  • Cable Pushdowns – Good tricep finishing movement
  • Barbell Squats – One of the best exercises for leg development
  • Romanian Deadlifts – Focuses on hamstrings
  • Leg Press – Allows heavy weight for leg strength
  • Lunges – Improves balance and coordination
  • Calf Raises – Targets lower leg muscles
  • Running – Burns a high number of calories
  • Cycling – Lower impact cardio option
  • Rowing Machine – Full body cardio workout
  • Stair Climber – Builds endurance and leg strength
  • Walking – Beginner friendly and easy to maintain
